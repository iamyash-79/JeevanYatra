Refund & Cancellation Policy

Our commitment to providing clear, fair, and transparent policies for your bookings.

1. Cancellation Policy

  • 80% refund if cancelled at least 7 days before the trip start date.
  • 60% refund if cancelled between 4 days before the trip.
  • 40% refund if cancelled between 2 days before the trip.
  • No refund for cancellations made within 48 hours of the trip.
  • All cancellations must be requested via email or our website form.

2. Refund Policy

  • Refunds will be processed within 7-10 business days after cancellation confirmation.
  • Refunds will be credited to the original payment method only.
  • Any bank charges or payment gateway fees are non-refundable.

3. Exceptions

  • In case of force majeure events (natural disasters, government restrictions, etc.), we will offer rescheduling instead of refunds.
  • Custom packages may have separate cancellation terms, mentioned at the time of booking.
